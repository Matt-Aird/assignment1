SmartGlass Project

The smart glass is a easy and convenient drinking glass that allows an easier way for bartenders and waiters to know when their customers need another refill on their drink. It will even allow you to ask for assistance not only for your drinks but for any help you need from your waiter. You can change what drink you wish to request at any time, just press the middle button and of a signal goes, to let the waiters know you need assistance.

The SmartGlass is very useful for waiters/waitresses and bartenders because it takes some of the stress away of always being rushed and constantly going back and forth between tables. Scientists have found that waiters and waitresses have a higher risk of a stroke than those with a low stress job by at least 22%. Miss hearing your customers request for a drink and accidently handing them the wrong drink is always stressful. The SmartGlass will help take that away by eliminating a huge factor is the miscommunication of ordering a drink. Making the job easier for the waiters/waitresses is essential for this item.

Features
  • The SmartGlass has 3 thin buttons on the bottom outter rim of the glass. The button furthest to the left is to request a refill of the same drink you ordered when you first arrived. The button in the middle is to request assistance, whether its to order more food, somethings wrong with the food or even if you would like to change the drink the your glass is set to. The button furthest to the right is to say no more drinks, this will stop the waiter/waitresses from coming to your table to ask if you want a refill when they see your glass is flashing red.
  • When your glass flashes red it means the glass is empty, it helps waiters/waitresses know that your glass is empty and they you may need another. If you are not needing another drink just press the button furthest to the right. There are two other colours that your glass will imitate. Your glass will shine green, which means that your glass is full, there for you do not need another drink just yet. The last colour is yellow, this shows when your glass is roughly half full, in this case most waiters/waitresses will often come up and ask if you would like a refill. the lights are also just for show, and a cool little feature.
  • Al though it is called a SmartGlass, its not actually made of glass. It is actually made of a firm thick plastic. This is for just incase it gets dropped, that way it wont shatter or crack. The waiter/waitresses have a monitor behind the bar. This is where all the data is recorded, it has the tables and the glasses on it, all showing what drinks go to what glasses. this also has the same colours that the SmartGlass has, showing whether a glass is full, half full, or empty. It also has a 'Tag' feature, this feature is incase a customer is too drunk and they have been cut off. it shuts off their light on the SmartGlass and also on the monitor with a sign that says 'Tagged'. This is to make sure that this person is not to drink an excessive amount.
  • In most cases, when you order a drink through the SmartGlass, the waiter/waitress would have made the drink and then bring it to you, rather than taking your glass away and filling it up, this speeds up the request for your drink so you are not waiting an excessive amount of time. The SmartGlass is also waterproof, this allows for easy cleaning, and well, its a drinking glass, so it would have to be liquid proof.
  • The last feature the SmartGlass has is a coaster, the coaster charges the glass whilst it is on top. the glass and coaster are connected by magnets to ensure that you do not knock over the glass.
  • All this is able to work because these glasses would be powered by Arduinos, using Arduino water sensors and basic buttons. These arduinos would send the data to the raspberry pi via Wifi, which is the information that the waiters see on the touch panel monitor. The Arduino water sensor would send the information to the cloud, and the raspberry pi connected to the monitors would send a pull protocol, which pulls the information from the cloud and then said information would appear onto the monitors. a raspberry pi is a small device that plugs into a tv/monitor and acts like a computer, displaying and outputting information.
